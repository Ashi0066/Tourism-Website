Trekking

There are many amazing trekking oportunities in Hunza Here are some famous ones

Mount Hunza and Lady Finger

Elevation : 2057ft

These are two of the famous mountains located in front of each other.This peak has two known routes used by british and swidish climbers back in 1991. Alpine style was used in both attempts

Mount Rakaposhi

Elevation : 25551ft

This is the 27th highest peak in the world.It is the only mountain with a 5000ft height from base camp to summit and it is the only mountain in the world which rises from fields to top.

Ultar Sar Peak

Elevation : 24239ft

This peak is near a beautiful village on the karakoram highway near karimabad a town in Hunza at once time this peak was considered to be the highest unclimed peak in the world which was debunked later. it is located nearby the Hunza Mount

Mount Diran

Elevation :23839ft

This mountain is considered to be the most dangerous mountain in Pakistna as it has caused many deaths in the Past. until 1964 expeditions attempts failed to reach the peak of this mountain but in 1968 some austrian climbers finally climbed it
